AWW-PAIRS Game 01







Note: This is a pre-release prototype, and we are maintaining secrecy about the idea and the progress so far. Please do not forward any links to this web game.

How to Play:

You should see a nice grid, looking something like this:

Click on the buttons in the grid. You should hear one of us speaking a name, of someone else in the group.
Somewhere in the grid, there are three buttons in a row, that share a special characteristic.
To win the game, find the three in a row, and send me a message that identifies which three it is, before anybody else solves it.
The three in a row can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

- - - -
The characteristics they might share are:

    1 - Three names spoken by the same person, or
    2 - The same name spoken by three different people, or
    3 - Three buttons where a person is saying their own name.

- - - -
Here are examples of the three characteristics, visually:

1. Marie saying Chuck, Marie saying Silas, Marie saying Diane
2. Les saying Grace, Ryan saying Grace, Kasia saying Grace
3. Kelly saying Kelly, Rhonda saying Rhonda, Jake saying Jake

Game 1 here is way easy, even people that don't know our voices could figure this one out. Just click on all the buttons and listen!
