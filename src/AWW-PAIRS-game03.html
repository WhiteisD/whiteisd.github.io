AWW-PAIRS Game 03






Note: This is a pre-release prototype, and we are maintaining secrecy about the idea and the progress so far. Please do not forward any links to this web game.

How to Play:

Click on the buttons in the grid. You should hear one of us speaking a name, of someone else in the group.

Today's game: "Pairings". This game is different from yesterday's.

Somewhere in the grid, there are two different "inverse pairs". An inverse pair is where, for instance, one tile has Joe saying Susan, and another tile has Susan saying Joe.
To win the game, find the two pairs, and send me a message that identifies where the four tiles are, before anybody else solves it.

This one is much harder than game #1. Only women's voices were selected for this go-round, and some of the lady's voices in our family are really similar! If you don't know our family's voices, you'd find this game near impossible. But if you don't know our voices and still want to give it a try, this primer and some general game play techniques might get you there!
